Apartament A

The apartamentâ€™s overall character has some references from the plain and light design lines of Scandinavian Indoor spaces with glimpses of a harmonious light multi-colour palette even though its different rooms have different specific character. The small living room divided by the dining room from retro, decorative, glass sliding-doors, is treated with some genuine, customized furniture and light harmonious colours. The dinning room was intended to give a sense of freshness and therefore treated with a light and sweet pastel colour and white. The main matrimonial room has some retro feeling, that can be clearly seen in some new furniture design elements and has a general sense of coziness and warmth. Also to achieve some character and naturality the main corridor is treated with light mint hues and bamboo elements.
