Apartament A

The apartment's overall character has some references from the plain and light design lines of Scandinavian Indoor spaces with glimpses of a harmonious light multi-color palette even though its different rooms have a different specific character. The small living room divided by the dining room from retro, decorative, glass sliding doors, is treated with some genuine, customized furniture and light harmonious colors. The dining room was intended to give a sense of freshness and therefore treated with a light and sweet pastel color and white. The main matrimonial room has some retro feeling, that can be clearly seen in some new furniture design elements and has a general sense of coziness and warmth. Also to achieve some character and naturality the main corridor is treated with light mint hues and bamboo elements.
