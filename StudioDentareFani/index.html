Dental studio Fani

The concept of the dental clinic is a result of the brief and request from the two clients, to have a space that would be a modern interpretation of the neo-classical style. The intent was to organize the small space we had, as rigorously as we could so that would be very functional and at the same time transmit a sense of delicacy, sweetness and ephemeral.
